## [PlanBench-XL: Evaluating Long-Horizon Planning of LLM Tool-Use Agents in Large-Scale Tool Ecosystems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22388)
**Authors:** (Authors not retrieved ‚Äî see arxiv link)
**TL;DR:** Introduces PlanBench-XL, an interactive benchmark of 327 retail tasks spanning 1,665 tools that tests whether agents can iteratively retrieve and invoke tools toward long-horizon goals. Experiments on ten leading LLMs show massive-tool planning remains hard: GPT-5.4 achieves 51.90% under normal conditions but collapses to 11.36% under the most severe blocking conditions, exposing a fundamental fragility in current planning architectures.

## [Agent Planning Benchmark (APB): A Diagnostic Framework for Planning Capabilities in LLM Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04874)
**Authors:** (Authors not retrieved ‚Äî see arxiv link)
**TL;DR:** APB is a planning-specific diagnostic benchmark with 4,209 multimodal cases across 22 domains and five settings, covering holistic planning, feedback-conditioned step-wise planning, and robustness under extraneous tools, broken tools, and unsolvable tasks. It fills a critical gap in evaluating whether LLM agents can decompose goals, select tools, reason over constraints, and recognize infeasible tasks.
