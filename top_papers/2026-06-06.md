## [SKIP: Sparse Keyframe Interpolation Paradigm for Efficient Embodied World Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.00702)

## [BitVLA: 1-bit Vision-Language-Action Models for Robotics Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.07530)
**Authors:** Hongyu Wang, Chuyan Xiong, Ruiping Wang, Xilin Chen
**TL;DR:** Introduces the first fully native 1-bit quantized VLA model for robotic manipulation, where every parameter is ternary {-1,0,1}, built on BitNet b1.58 2B4T. ## [LeVERB: Humanoid Whole-Body Control with Latent Vision-Language Instruction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.13751)
**Authors:** Xue Linfeng et al. (UC Berkeley)
**TL;DR:** Presents a framework for whole-body humanoid control driven by latent representations of vision-language instructions, bridging high-level natural language commands with low-level whole-body motor control. Achieves competitive performance on humanoid whole-body benchmarks.
