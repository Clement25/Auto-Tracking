# Top AI News (2026-06-27)

## [OpenAI Launches Limited GPT-5.6 Preview with Three-Tier Model Series](https://openai.com/news/)
**Source:** TechCrunch / VentureBeat
**Summary:** OpenAI quietly launched a limited preview of its GPT-5.6 series on June 26, offering three tiers — Sol (complex tasks), Terra (daily scenarios), and Luna (low-cost large-scale calls) — currently available only to a small number of partner institutions. The announcement came after Polymarket odds for a broader launch by June 28 collapsed from 83% to ~18%, signaling a shift of the full rollout to July and intensifying competitive pressure.
