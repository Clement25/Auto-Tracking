## [Canada's Privacy Commissioner Rules OpenAI Violated Privacy Law in Building ChatGPT](https://openai.com/news/)
**Source:** OpenAI / Reuters
**Summary:** Canada's Office of the Privacy Commissioner issued findings that OpenAI violated Canadian privacy law in the development of ChatGPT, citing overcollection of personal information without proportionality assessment, lack of valid consent for scraped training data, and inadequate safeguards for sensitive personal data. The ruling adds regulatory pressure from a G7 country on top of existing EU and US scrutiny, and may force changes to how frontier model developers source training data.
