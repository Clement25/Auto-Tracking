## [Midjourney Wants Hollywood Studios to Reveal Details of Their Own AI Usage](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/04/midjourney-wants-hollywood-studios-to-reveal-the-details-of-their-ai-usage/)
**Source:** TechCrunch
**Summary:** AI image startup Midjourney is seeking to compel Disney, Universal, and Warner Bros. to disclose their own generative AI usage as part of ongoing copyright litigation. A judge had ruled studios must provide information only where AI led to "consumer-facing" outputs; Midjourney's new filing seeks to overturn that narrow limitation. The case matters because studios suing AI companies for copyright infringement while quietly using AI themselves creates a major double-standard that could reshape how courts treat AI-generated content.
