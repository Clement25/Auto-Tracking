## [Pope Leo XIV Signs First Encyclical on AI: "Magnifica Humanitas"](https://www.transparencycoalition.ai/news/ai-legislative-update-may29-2026)
**Source:** Transparency Coalition / Vatican
**Summary:** Pope Leo XIV signed "Magnifica Humanitas: On Safeguarding the Human Person in the Time of Artificial Intelligence," the first papal encyclical dedicated entirely to AI ethics, framing the technology through five principles: common good, universal destination of goods, subsidiarity, solidarity, and social justice. The document argues that technology is never neutral and calls on governments and corporations to ensure AI benefits are distributed equitably rather than concentrating power. The encyclical is expected to influence Catholic-majority nations' policy debates and has already drawn responses from EU and UN AI ethics bodies.
