## [Pope Leo XIV Releases AI Encyclical "Magnifica Humanitas" Alongside Anthropic Co-Founder](https://www.vaticannews.va/en/pope/news/2026-05/pope-leo-xiv-first-encyclical-magnifica-humanitas.html)
**Source:** Vatican News / CNN / National Catholic Reporter
**Summary:** Pope Leo XIV released his first encyclical, *Magnifica Humanitas* ("Magnificent Humanity"), on May 25 alongside Anthropic co-founder Chris Olah—the first papal document focused entirely on AI and the protection of the human person. Signed exactly 135 years after Pope Leo XIII's *Rerum Novarum* on workers' rights, the 235-page document explicitly frames AI as the Industrial Revolution of our time, calls for robust external regulation of the private sector's concentration of AI power, and warns against AI being weaponized for warfare or surveillance.

## [Thinking Machines Lab Unveils Full-Duplex "Interaction Models" with 0.4-Second Response](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/11/thinking-machines-wants-to-build-an-ai-that-actually-listens-while-it-talks/)
**Source:** TechCrunch / MarkTechPost
**Summary:** Thinking Machines Lab (founded by former OpenAI CTO Mira Murati) announced TML-Interaction-Small, a 276B-parameter mixture-of-experts model built natively for full-duplex real-time interaction—it processes input and generates output simultaneously in 200ms blocks. The model responds in 0.40 seconds (human conversational speed) and outperforms GPT-Realtime-2.0 and Gemini on FD-bench v1.5 (77.8 vs. 54.3 and 47.8). This represents a fundamental architectural departure from turn-based LLMs toward truly concurrent human-AI interaction.

## [Light-Matter Particle Breakthrough Could Dramatically Speed Up AI Computing](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/05/260518041341.htm)
**Source:** ScienceDaily / MIT Technology Review
**Summary:** Researchers at the University of Pennsylvania created a novel hybrid light-matter particle (polariton-based system) that can be used to perform AI computations at the speed of light while consuming far less energy than conventional silicon-based AI accelerators.
