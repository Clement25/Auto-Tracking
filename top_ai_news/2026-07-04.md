## [Meituan Open-Sources LongCat-2.0: 1.6T-Parameter Model Trained Entirely on Chinese Chips](https://venturebeat.com/technology/meituan-open-sources-longcat-2-0-the-1-6t-near-frontier-agentic-coding-model-thats-been-leading-openrouter-trained-entirely-on-chinese-chips)
**Source:** VentureBeat
**Summary:** Chinese food-delivery giant Meituan open-sourced LongCat-2.0, a 1.6-trillion-parameter Mixture-of-Experts coding model trained on a 50,000-card cluster of domestic Chinese ASICs — making it the largest model to complete both pre-training and inference entirely on non-NVIDIA hardware. Released under MIT license with a 1M-token context window, it had been quietly topping OpenRouter's usage charts as "Owl Alpha" and scores near-frontier alongside GPT-5.5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro. This is a landmark proof point that China can train frontier-scale models despite US chip export controls, with significant geopolitical implications for the global AI supply chain.
