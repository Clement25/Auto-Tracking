## [China's Moonshot AI Releases Kimi K3 — Largest Open-Source Model Ever](https://venturebeat.com/technology/chinas-moonshot-ai-releases-kimi-k3-the-largest-open-source-model-ever-rivaling-top-u-s-systems)
**Source:** VentureBeat
**Summary:** Moonshot AI launched Kimi K3, a sparse Mixture-of-Experts model with ~2.8 trillion total parameters — roughly 75% larger than DeepSeek V4 Pro. It features a 1-million-token context window, native vision, and an always-on "thinking mode," scoring third globally on GDPval-AA v2 behind only Claude Fable 5 Max and GPT-5.6 Sol Max. Full open weights release is scheduled for July 27 under a Modified MIT license, signaling that China has reached genuine frontier model parity and setting a new open-weights standard.

## [Anthropic Discovers "J-Space" Inside Claude — A Hidden Reasoning Workspace](https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropics-new-j-lens-reveals-a-silent-workspace-inside-claude-that-mirrors-a-leading-theory-of-consciousness)
**Source:** VentureBeat / MIT Technology Review
**Summary:** Anthropic's researchers built a new interpretability tool called the Jacobian lens (J-lens) that revealed a "J-space" — a small, privileged internal zone inside Claude Opus 4.6 where the model holds concepts it can reason with, surrounded by a much larger ocean of automatic processing. The structure closely parallels "global workspace theory" from neuroscience, which describes how conscious thought works in the human brain. This is the most significant AI interpretability finding in years, providing a concrete tool for monitoring internal states for safety risks and reigniting serious scientific debate about AI consciousness.

## [Meet GPT-Red: An LLM Super-Hacker OpenAI Built to Make Its Models Safer](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/07/15/1140514/meet-gpt-red-an-llm-super-hacker-openai-built-to-make-its-models-safer/)
**Source:** MIT Technology Review
**Summary:** OpenAI released details on GPT-Red, a model trained via self-play reinforcement learning to automatically red-team its other AI systems — simultaneously training attacker and defender models, where GPT-Red is rewarded for finding exploits like prompt injections while defenders are rewarded for resisting. GPT-Red discovered novel "fake chain of thought" injection attacks, and against GPT-5.6, attack success rates dropped from over 90% to under 23%. Automated red-teaming at this scale is a major step forward for AI safety infrastructure as agents become more autonomous and adversarially exposed.

## [Satya Nadella Warns: Companies "Pay for Intelligence Twice" Using AI](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/13/satya-nadella-has-issued-a-shocking-warning-to-companies-using-ai/)
**Source:** TechCrunch
**Summary:** Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella coined the "Reverse Information Paradox" — warning that enterprises unknowingly hand over their most valuable institutional know-how every time they use proprietary AI models, as the models learn from prompts, tool usage, and user corrections. He urged enterprises to build proprietary learning environments and use orchestration layers to avoid vendor lock-in. Coming from OpenAI's largest partner, the warning signals a strategic pivot positioning Microsoft as the "safe" AI choice while framing rival labs as data-extractive risks.

## [Xi Jinping Launches Global AI Governance Alliance (WAICO)](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/17/chinas-xi-jinping-launches-new-ai-alliance-what-is-it)
**Source:** Al Jazeera / CNBC
**Summary:** Chinese President Xi Jinping delivered the opening keynote at the 2026 World AI Conference in Shanghai, launching the inter-governmental World AI Cooperation Organization (WAICO) with 29 founding member countries including Indonesia, Brazil, Malaysia, South Africa, and Russia. Xi pitched China as an open AI partner to the developing world while warning against excessive security restrictions. WAICO is a direct geopolitical counter to US-led AI governance frameworks, building an alternative AI standards coalition that could shape regulation and infrastructure deployment across a large portion of the world.
