**Summary:** Google, Microsoft, Salesforce, Snowflake, and ServiceNow announced support for a shared protocol for connecting AI agents to business software, explicitly framed as a response to Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP), which has become the de facto standard for agent-tool integration.
