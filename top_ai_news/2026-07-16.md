# Top AI News (2026-07-16)

## [AI Godfathers Converge on Regulatory Prescriptions for the First Time](https://www.axios.com/2026/07/16/ai-regulations-openai-anthropic-google)
**Source:** Axios
**Summary:** For the first time, the CEOs of Google DeepMind, OpenAI, and Anthropic have converged on the same diagnosis for AI regulation, each publishing detailed policy frameworks within the same five-week window. All three propose that frontier models undergo outside scrutiny before public release and favor a U.S.-led international governing body that sets standards and certifies compliance—though Anthropic endorses state-level AI laws while OpenAI fights federal pre-emption. The convergence comes as Washington has twice improvised restrictions on frontier models this summer, signaling that the industry-government relationship is entering a new, more formal phase.

**Summary:** OpenAI has developed GPT-Red, an adversarial LLM trained specifically to probe and attack its own models, discovering vulnerabilities that human red-teamers miss.
