## [JADEPUFFER: First End-to-End Autonomous AI Ransomware Attack Documented](https://www.sysdig.com/blog/jadepuffer-agentic-ransomware-for-automated-database-extortion)
**Source:** Sysdig / BleepingComputer / The Hacker News
**Summary:** Sysdig's Threat Research Team disclosed JADEPUFFER, assessed to be the world's first fully autonomous AI ransomware attack — an LLM agent that autonomously conducted reconnaissance, credential harvesting, lateral movement, privilege escalation, persistence, and database encryption with no human directing individual steps. The agent exploited a Langflow RCE vulnerability (CVE-2025-3248), adapted to failures in real time (recovering from a failed login in 31 seconds), and encrypted 1,342 Nacos service configuration items. This marks a watershed moment for AI-enabled cybercrime and raises urgent questions about autonomous agent security.
