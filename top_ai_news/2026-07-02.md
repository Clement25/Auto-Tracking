## [Anthropic Redeploys Claude Fable 5 Globally After US Lifts Export Controls, Adds Cybersecurity Classifier](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/01/anthropic-redeploys-claude-fable-5-on-july-1-after-us-export-controls-lift-adds-new-cybersecurity-classifier/)
**Source:** MarkTechPost / VentureBeat / Al Jazeera
**Summary:** Anthropic restored global access to Claude Fable 5 on July 1 after the US Commerce Department (Secretary Howard Lutnick) lifted the June 12 export-control order, requiring the company to proactively detect and address security risks.
