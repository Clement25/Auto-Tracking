**Summary:** The Trump administration issued an export-control directive on June 12, requiring Anthropic to suspend worldwide access to its flagship Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models, citing a reported "narrow, non-universal jailbreak" in which a researcher prompted the model to identify previously known software vulnerabilities.
