## [Colorado Scraps June 30 AI Enforcement Deadline; New Law Takes Effect January 2027](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/318002/20260608/colorados-ai-law-takes-effect-june-30-it-gives-you-right-appeal-decision-ai-made-about-you.htm)
**Source:** TechTimes / Hunton Privacy Blog
**Summary:** Colorado's landmark SB24-205 AI Act—billed as the first high-risk AI enforcement deadline on US soil—has been effectively replaced before it could take effect. The state legislature passed SB 26-189 on May 9, 2026 (signed by Governor Polis), rewriting the law to focus on disclosure, explanation, and a consumer right to request human review of consequential automated decisions, with the new framework taking effect January 1, 2027. The change eliminates the original duty-of-care and algorithmic discrimination prevention requirements, reflecting industry pushback and legislative recalibration on AI regulation.
