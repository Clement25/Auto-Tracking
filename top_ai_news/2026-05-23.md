**Summary:** A widely anticipated AI executive order was abruptly cancelled hours before President Trump was set to sign it, following 11th-hour calls from AI/crypto czar David Sacks and tech industry CEOs. ## [OpenAI Model Autonomously Disproves 80-Year-Old Geometry Conjecture](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-may-23-2026)

**Summary:** OpenAI's internal model independently disproved a geometry conjecture that had stood for 80 years, with Fields medalist Tim Gowers calling it "a milestone in AI mathematics."
