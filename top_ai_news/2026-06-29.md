## [US Government vs. Anthropic: Federal Judge Blocks Trump's Designation of AI Company as Security Risk](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/openai-anthropic-new-ai-spending-reality-as-users-shift-to-efficiency.html)
**Source:** CNBC / Multiple outlets
**Summary:** Following a February 2026 DOD demand that Anthropic allow Claude for "all lawful purposes" — including lethal autonomous weapons and mass domestic surveillance — Anthropic refused and drew two public red lines. President Trump responded by ordering all federal agencies to cease use of Anthropic's technology, and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth designated Anthropic a supply chain risk; a federal judge subsequently granted Anthropic an injunction ordering the government to rescind the designation. The standoff is shaping an important precedent for whether the government can compel AI companies to enable military uses they consider unethical.
