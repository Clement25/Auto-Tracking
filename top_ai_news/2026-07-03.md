## [Morgan Stanley Cuts Riskiest Reconciliation Work in Half — By Making AI Agents Less Autonomous](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/morgan-stanley-cut-its-riskiest-reconciliation-job-in-half-by-making-its-agents-less-autonomous)
**Source:** VentureBeat / CNBC
**Summary:** Morgan Stanley deployed AI agents in profit-and-loss reconciliation and cut the workload in half — not by maximizing autonomy, but by keeping humans tightly in the loop and iteratively encoding their decisions as repeatable rules. The story is being cited as a template for high-stakes enterprise agent deployment: fewer probabilistic decisions, more fixed rules, human sign-off on critical calls.
