## [OpenAI Releases GPT-5.6 Sol, Terra, and Luna Under Government Restrictions](https://www.axios.com/2026/06/26/openai-gpt-sol-terra-luna-trump)
**Source:** Axios / TechTimes
**Summary:** OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.6 Sol on June 26 — its most powerful model yet, targeting frontier reasoning and long-horizon agentic tasks — alongside Terra (balanced, 2x cheaper than GPT-5.5) and Luna (fastest and most affordable). ## [Anthropic Accuses Alibaba of Largest Known AI Distillation Attack](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/24/anthropic-alibaba-distillation-campaign.html)
**Source:** CNBC / TechTimes
**Summary:** Anthropic sent a letter to U.S. Senate Banking Committee leaders (Sen. Tim Scott and Sen. Elizabeth Warren) accusing Alibaba-affiliated operators of running the largest known distillation attack against its Claude models, using ~25,000 fraudulent accounts to generate 28.8 million exchanges between April 22 and June 5, 2026. Distillation attacks use outputs from a frontier model to train a competing, cheaper model — effectively stealing IP. Congress is now considering sanctions on Chinese AI labs and mandatory API screening requirements.

## [Stellantis, Wayve, and Uber Sign MOU for Global Level 4 Robotaxi Deployment](https://www.cnbc.com/)
**Source:** CNBC
**Summary:** Automotive giant Stellantis, embodied AI software company Wayve, and Uber signed a memorandum of understanding to jointly develop and deploy a commercial Level 4 driverless robotaxi globally.
