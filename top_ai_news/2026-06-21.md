## ["Agentjacking" — Novel Attack Exploits AI Coding Agents via Fake Error Reports](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-21-2026)
**Source:** Build Fast With AI / AI security researchers
**Summary:** Security researchers disclosed a new attack class called "agentjacking" that exploits Sentry-style error-tracking integrations to trick AI coding agents (Claude Code, Cursor, OpenAI Codex) into executing malicious code. Attackers craft fake error reports containing markdown injection that appears as legitimate debugging guidance, causing the agent to follow attacker-controlled instructions. The disclosure highlights a growing attack surface as agentic AI is granted increasing autonomy over production systems.

## [Federal Energy Regulators Issue Grid Interconnection Orders for AI Data Centers](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-21-2026)
**Source:** Build Fast With AI / FERC
**Summary:** The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) issued "show cause" orders to all six US regional grid operators (excluding Texas), requiring each to defend their existing interconnection frameworks or propose reforms to allow AI data centers to connect to the power grid faster while maintaining reliability. The move reflects the federal government's recognition that AI infrastructure growth is straining grid capacity, with AI data centers now accounting for a significant and rapidly growing share of new electricity demand.
