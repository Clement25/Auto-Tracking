## [Cadence and NVIDIA Expand Partnership to Close the Sim-to-Real Gap in Robotics](https://www.nvidia.com/gtc/)
**Source:** NVIDIA / GTC 2026
**Summary:** Cadence Design Systems and NVIDIA announced an expanded partnership combining Cadence's high-fidelity simulation engines with NVIDIA's Isaac robotics libraries and Cosmos world-model foundation models. The integration targets the "sim-to-real" gap — the persistent performance degradation when policies trained in simulation are deployed on physical robots. This is a key enabling partnership for scaling embodied AI development without requiring prohibitive amounts of physical robot-hours.
