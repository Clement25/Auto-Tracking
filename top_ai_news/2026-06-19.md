## [OpenAI Acquires Astral, Maker of Python Tools uv, Ruff, and ty](https://openai.com/index/openai-to-acquire-astral/)

**Summary:** OpenAI acquired Astral, the team behind the Rust-written Python developer tools uv (dependency management), Ruff (linting/formatting), and ty (type checking), integrating the team directly into its Codex division.
