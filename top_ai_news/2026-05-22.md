## [Andrej Karpathy Joins Anthropic's Pre-Training Team](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/19/openai-co-founder-andrej-karpathy-joins-anthropics-pre-training-team/)

## [Trump White House Pulls AI Executive Order on Pre-Release Government Review](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/22/ai-executive-order-cancelled-white-house)
**Source:** Axios / Washington Post / CNN
**Summary:** The White House postponed — and ultimately shelved — a planned executive order that would have established a voluntary framework for AI companies to submit advanced models to federal agencies for security review (up to 90 days) before public release. President Trump stated he "didn't like certain aspects" of the order; the move came after lobbying from major AI firms including OpenAI and Anthropic. The cancellation leaves a regulatory vacuum around frontier AI model governance and creates uncertainty heading into the IPO season.

## [Bureau of Labor Statistics Reports AI-Driven Employment Decline Across 18 Professions](https://fortune.com/2026/05/12/how-ai-layoffs-compare-china-shock-donald-trump-nafta/)
**Source:** Fortune / BLS
**Summary:** The Bureau of Labor Statistics quietly reported that employment across 18 AI-impacted professions declined from May 2024 to May 2025, with sales representatives seeing the sharpest drop at -4.8%. Economists are drawing parallels to the "China shock" of the early 2000s, when trade disruption hollowed out manufacturing communities. Unlike factory job losses concentrated in specific geographies, AI-driven displacement appears to be spreading into service and knowledge-worker roles in urban centers.
