## [Trump Signs Narrower AI Executive Order After Industry Pushback](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/02/trump-signs-narrower-executive-order-on-ai-oversight-after-industry-objections/)
**Source:** TechCrunch / MIT Technology Review
**Summary:** President Trump signed a revised AI executive order on June 2 requiring only voluntary pre-release government reviews of frontier AI models — 30 days before launch, down from an earlier draft that called for 90 days — with no mandatory licensing or permitting requirements. The EO also directs the DOJ to treat AI-assisted cybercrime as a high-priority enforcement area and establishes an AI cybersecurity clearinghouse, representing a lighter-touch federal AI governance approach after significant lobbying from the tech industry.

## [Microsoft Launches MXC: An OS-Level Sandbox for AI Agents](https://venturebeat.com/security/microsoft-launches-mxc-an-os-level-sandbox-for-ai-agents-with-openai-and-nvidia-already-on-board/)
**Source:** VentureBeat
**Summary:** Microsoft unveiled Microsoft Execution Containers (MXC), a policy-driven execution layer built into Windows itself that lets developers and IT administrators declare exactly what an AI agent can and cannot access, with boundaries enforced at runtime by the OS kernel. With OpenAI and Nvidia already on board as launch partners, MXC represents a fundamental shift toward OS-level agent security governance, addressing enterprise concerns about agentic AI running amok.
