## [AI Fact-Checking Dependency Makes People Worse at Detecting Misinformation](https://news.mit.edu/2026/consequences-of-relying-on-ai-for-accurate-news-0609)

**Summary:** An MIT study found that participants who relied on AI chatbots to verify facts over a month performed significantly worse at detecting misinformation independently once the AI was removed.
