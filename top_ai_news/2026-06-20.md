## [Trump Signs Executive Order on AI Innovation and Security](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/06/promoting-advanced-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-security/)
**Source:** The White House / Scientific American
**Summary:** President Trump signed an executive order requiring AI companies to voluntarily share new frontier models with the US government for up to 30 days before public release, framing it as both an innovation-enablement and national-security measure. The order represents a significant pivot from the previous administration's approach and sparked immediate debate about regulatory overreach versus the need for government AI readiness — particularly given ongoing Pentagon evaluations of OpenAI and Google models for classified systems.
