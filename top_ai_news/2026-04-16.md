## [This Simulation Startup Wants to Be the Cursor for Physical AI](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/16/this-simulation-startup-wants-to-be-the-cursor-for-physical-ai/)
**Source:** TechCrunch
**Summary:** A New York-based startup (co-founded by ex-Meta Reality Labs and Google DeepMind engineers) raised an $8.5M seed round to build developer tooling for physical AI — letting autonomous agents iterate on robotic and physical autonomy systems inside software simulators before deployment. ## [Meta Debuts Muse Spark, First Major AI Model Under Alexandr Wang](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/meta-debuts-first-major-ai-model-since-14-billion-deal-to-bring-in-alexandr-wang.html)
**Source:** CNBC
**Summary:** Meta unveiled Muse Spark, its first proprietary AI model developed under Scale AI founder Alexandr Wang, who joined Meta as part of a $14.3B deal. Unlike Meta's previous open-source Llama line, Muse Spark is closed-weight and powers the Meta AI app across Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp with voice, text, and image inputs. A "Contemplating" mode deploys a squad of parallel reasoning agents for complex queries, competing with OpenAI's GPT-Pro and Google's Gemini Deep Think.

## [Improving the Academic Workflow: Introducing Two AI Agents for Better Figures and Peer Review](https://research.google/blog/improving-the-academic-workflow-introducing-two-ai-agents-for-better-figures-and-peer-review/)
**Source:** Google Research Blog
