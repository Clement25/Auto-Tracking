# Top AI News (2026-06-22)

## [Noam Shazeer, Transformer Co-Author, Leaves Google DeepMind for OpenAI](https://theaiinsider.tech/2026/06/19/openai-hires-transformer-co-author-noam-shazeer-and-former-white-house-ai-official-dean-ball-ahead-of-ipo/)
**Source:** TechCrunch / The AI Insider
**Summary:** Noam Shazeer, co-author of the 2017 landmark paper "Attention Is All You Need" that introduced the Transformer architecture, has left Google DeepMind to join OpenAI as Lead for Architecture Research. Shazeer had previously co-founded Character AI and was a key figure in Google's Gemini project before this surprise departure. The move is seen as a major talent coup for OpenAI ahead of its anticipated IPO, and represents one of the most significant defections in AI industry history given Shazeer's foundational role in modern LLM development.

## [Trump Signs AI Executive Order Requiring 30-Day Government Preview of Frontier Models](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/06/promoting-advanced-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-security/)
**Source:** The White House / CNBC
**Summary:** President Trump signed an executive order titled "Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security," directing federal agencies to establish a framework for secure frontier AI deployment, including a voluntary process by which AI developers would give the US government up to 30 days of early access to frontier models before releasing to other partners. The EO also directs DHS and CISA to issue new operational guidance protecting federal networks from AI-enabled cyber threats within 30 days. This marks the most significant federal AI governance action of 2026 and sets the stage for deeper government involvement in AI model oversight.
