## [OpenAI Claims It Solved an 80-Year-Old Math Problem — For Real This Time](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/20/openai-claims-it-solved-an-80-year-old-math-problem-for-real-this-time/)
**Source:** TechCrunch
**Summary:** OpenAI's internal general-purpose reasoning model autonomously disproved the Erdős planar unit distance conjecture — an open problem posed in 1946 — by discovering new constructions from algebraic number theory that beat square grids. The proof was independently verified by mathematicians Noga Alon, Thomas Bloom, Melanie Wood, and Will Sawin. This marks the first time an AI has autonomously resolved a prominent open problem in mathematics without task-specific fine-tuning.
