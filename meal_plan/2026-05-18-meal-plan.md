## Nearby Restaurant Guide

### Near Home (Margaret Drive / Queenstown)

- **Hello Fish 哈喽鱼 (Margaret Drive Hawker Centre, 38A Margaret Drive)** — HK-style fresh fish steamed to order (tilapia, grouper, sea bass), served as a complete set with steamed rice and a side vegetable. ~450 kcal, ~38g protein. Minimal oil, zero chilli by default, clean high-quality protein. *Use Tue & Sun.*

- **Nine Miles Chicken Stew (Margaret Drive Hawker Centre, 38A Margaret Drive)** — Authentic Cantonese-style chicken congee and century-egg porridge. ~350–400 kcal, ~22–28g protein. Light, gently warming, easy on digestion; naturally low in fat. *Use Fri.*

- **Hakka Thunder Tea Rice (Margaret Drive Hawker Centre, 38A Margaret Drive)** — Signature Hakka dish: stir-fried cabbage, spinach, firm tofu, peanuts, and anchovies over brown rice; served with an herbal green-tea broth. ~460 kcal, ~20g protein. High in fibre, antioxidants, and plant-based protein; very low in saturated fat. Request brown rice and extra tofu. *Use Wed.*

- **Urban Folks (Margaret Market, 38 Margaret Drive)** — Aussie-style café with grain bowls, sandwiches and acai bowls. Grain bowl with grilled chicken ~470 kcal, ~32g protein. Skip spicy aioli; ask for dressing on the side. *Use Sat.*

### Near Office (Buona Vista / The Metropolis)

- **SaladStop! (The Metropolis #01-16, 9 North Buona Vista Drive)** — Build-your-own salad or grain bowl. Choose grilled chicken or salmon, quinoa, edamame, olive-oil/lemon dressing. ~480–500 kcal, ~35–38g protein. Full macro transparency on the menu. *Use Mon.*

- **Wild Crumbs (The Metropolis, 9 North Buona Vista Drive)** — Japanese-style rice bowls and sandos. Grilled chicken or salmon rice bowl with edamame and pickled vegetables. ~420–440 kcal, ~30–34g protein. Request no chilli oil. *Use Tue.*

- **The Soup Spoon Union (The Metropolis, 9 North Buona Vista Drive)** — Gourmet broth-based soups at ~300–350 kcal per bowl; pair with a whole-grain roll for ~380 kcal. Choose clear or tomato-based soups over cream varieties. *Use Thu.*

- **Ikki Izakaya (The Metropolis #01-06/07, 9 North Buona Vista Drive)** — Casual Japanese eatery with grilled chicken and salmon sets, miso soup and garden salad. ~500–520 kcal, ~36–40g protein. Request teriyaki sauce on the side; no chilli. *Use Fri.*

- **YOLO (Star Vista, 1 Vista Exchange Green #01-06, ~8 min walk)** — Cauliflower Rice with Herb Chicken (~320 kcal) and Brown Rice with Salmon (~480 kcal). Calorie counts on menu. Ask staff to replace kimchi with steamed greens to keep it non-spicy. *Alternate lunch option any day.*

## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Scrambled Eggs with Avocado & Toast
- **Ingredients:** 3 eggs, 1 slice whole-grain bread, ¼ avocado, cherry tomatoes, ½ tsp olive oil, salt, pepper
- **Prep time:** 10 min
- **Method:** Whisk eggs, cook in non-stick pan over low heat with minimal oil. Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon juice. Layer eggs on toast, top with tomatoes.
- **Macros:** ~420 kcal | 28g protein | 25g carbs | 22g fat

### Overnight Oats with Whey Protein
- **Ingredients:** 40g rolled oats, 200ml unsweetened almond milk, 1 scoop vanilla whey protein, 1 banana, 1 tsp honey
- **Prep time:** 5 min (+ overnight soak)
- **Method:** Combine oats, milk, and protein powder in a jar; stir well. Refrigerate overnight. Top with sliced banana in the morning.
- **Macros:** ~420 kcal | 30g protein | 55g carbs | 9g fat

### Egg-White Omelette with Mushrooms & Spinach
- **Ingredients:** 4 egg whites, 1 whole egg, 80g sliced mushrooms, 50g baby spinach, 1 slice whole-grain toast, 1 tsp olive oil, salt, pepper
- **Prep time:** 12 min
- **Method:** Beat eggs and whites. Sauté mushrooms and spinach in olive oil until wilted. Pour egg mixture over, fold once set. Serve with toasted bread.
- **Macros:** ~380 kcal | 30g protein | 28g carbs | 12g fat

### Meal-Prep Grilled Chicken Rice Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 120g chicken breast, ½ cup uncooked brown rice, 150g mixed vegetables (broccoli, snap peas, carrots), 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil, 2 cloves garlic
- **Prep time:** 25 min (batch-cook on Sunday for Wed)
- **Method:** Marinate chicken in soy sauce + garlic, pan-sear 6 min each side. Steam rice. Steam or stir-fry vegetables with garlic. Assemble, drizzle sesame oil.
- **Macros:** ~480 kcal | 38g protein | 50g carbs | 10g fat

### Pan-Seared Salmon with Broccoli & Brown Rice
- **Ingredients:** 150g salmon fillet, 150g broccoli florets, ½ cup cooked brown rice, 1 tsp olive oil, ½ lemon, salt, dried dill
- **Prep time:** 20 min
- **Method:** Season salmon with salt, pepper, and dill. Sear in hot pan 4 min per side. Steam broccoli until tender-crisp. Serve over rice with lemon squeeze.
- **Macros:** ~520 kcal | 38g protein | 35g carbs | 18g fat

### Protein Smoothie
- **Ingredients:** 1 scoop vanilla whey/plant protein, 200ml unsweetened almond milk, 1 banana, large handful baby spinach, 3–4 ice cubes
- **Prep time:** 5 min
- **Method:** Add all ingredients to blender; blend 30 seconds until smooth.
- **Macros:** ~350 kcal | 30g protein | 45g carbs | 6g fat

### Steamed Chicken Thigh with Bok Choy & Brown Rice
- **Ingredients:** 120g boneless skinless chicken thigh, 150g bok choy, ½ cup cooked brown rice, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tsp oyster sauce, 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
- **Prep time:** 20 min
- **Method:** Place chicken on a plate, steam 18–20 min until cooked through. Stir-fry bok choy in a little water with garlic for 3 min. Serve over rice with sauces drizzled on top.
- **Macros:** ~480 kcal | 36g protein | 40g carbs | 13g fat

### Canned Tuna Salad Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 120g canned tuna in water (drained), large handful mixed greens, 10 cherry tomatoes, ½ cucumber, ¼ red onion, 1 tbsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, salt, pepper
- **Prep time:** 8 min
- **Method:** Flake drained tuna into a bowl. Add greens, halved tomatoes, sliced cucumber, thinly sliced onion. Dress with olive oil and lemon. Season to taste.
- **Macros:** ~350 kcal | 38g protein | 12g carbs | 14g fat

### Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs
- **Ingredients:** 2 slices whole-grain bread, ½ ripe avocado, 2 eggs, squeeze of lemon, salt, black pepper
- **Prep time:** 12 min
- **Method:** Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt. Poach eggs 3–4 min in gently simmering water. Top toasts with mashed avocado and a poached egg each.
- **Macros:** ~480 kcal | 22g protein | 40g carbs | 26g fat

### Sunday Meal-Prep Power Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 150g chicken breast, ½ cup uncooked brown rice, 100g sweet potato, 150g broccoli, 1 tsp olive oil, herbs (rosemary, thyme), salt
- **Prep time:** 30 min
- **Method:** Roast sweet potato cubes at 200°C for 20 min with olive oil and herbs. Grill chicken breast. Steam rice and broccoli. Assemble in a bowl.
- **Macros:** ~550 kcal | 45g protein | 60g carbs | 12g fat
