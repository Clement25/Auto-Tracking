- **Seeds Cafe** — 501 Margaret Drive, Rainbow Centre · _Miso Chicken Warm Bowl_ ~400 kcal · High-protein, low-fat broth-based warm bowl; request without chilli flakes. Great for a light dinner.
- **Qi Philosophy** — Queenstown (approx. 0.8 km) · _Basmati Rice Bowl with Grilled Chicken_ ~450 kcal · Uses low-GI basmati rice; rich in fibre and protein; vegan/vegetarian options also available. Request no spicy seasoning.
- **Margaret Market** — 4 Margaret Drive (ground floor food hall) · _Steamed Fish + Stir-fried Vegetables_ ~350–450 kcal · Multiple hawker stalls; choose steamed or boiled preparations; skip deep-fried items and chilli sauces. Good weekend dinner option.

- **SaladStop!** — Near The Metropolis / one-north area · _Custom Grain Bowl (grilled chicken + quinoa + greens)_ ~480–550 kcal · Fully customisable; choose vinaigrette over cream dressings; skip croutons. High protein, high fibre.

## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Scrambled Eggs + Avocado Toast (Mon Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 3 large eggs, 2 slices whole-grain bread, ½ ripe avocado, pinch of salt, pepper, and dried herbs
- **Prep time:** 8 min
- Whisk eggs, cook on low heat with minimal olive oil spray. Toast bread. Mash avocado on toast, top with eggs.

### Pan-Seared Chicken + Sweet Potato + Bok Choy (Mon Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 180g chicken breast, 1 medium sweet potato, 150g bok choy, olive oil, garlic (optional), lemon, soy sauce (light)
- **Prep time:** 25 min
- Season chicken with salt, pepper, dried thyme. Sear 5–6 min each side. Cube sweet potato, roast 200°C for 20 min. Steam bok choy 3 min, toss in light soy sauce.

### Oat Porridge + Boiled Eggs (Tue Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** ½ cup rolled oats, 1 cup almond milk, 2 eggs, ½ cup blueberries, pinch cinnamon
- **Prep time:** 10 min
- Simmer oats in almond milk 5 min with cinnamon. Boil eggs 7–8 min for jammy yolk. Serve with berries.

### Tuna-Quinoa Bowl (Tue Lunch — Packed)
- **Ingredients:** 1 can tuna in water (drained, ~100g), ½ cup cooked quinoa, ½ cucumber (sliced), handful cherry tomatoes, juice of ½ lemon, 1 tsp olive oil, salt, pepper
- **Prep time:** 10 min (+ overnight quinoa cook)
- Mix all ingredients in a container. Pack cold; dress with lemon and oil just before eating.

### Egg-White Omelet (Wed Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 4 egg whites + 1 whole egg, handful spinach, 5 button mushrooms (sliced), salt, pepper, spray oil
- **Prep time:** 10 min
- Whisk eggs together, cook in sprayed non-stick pan on medium heat. Add vegetables, fold omelet.

### Baked Cod + Quinoa Salad (Wed Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 180g cod fillet, ½ cup cooked quinoa, ½ cucumber (diced), 10 cherry tomatoes (halved), 1 tsp olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, 150g asparagus
- **Prep time:** 20 min
- Bake cod at 200°C for 12–15 min with lemon slices and herbs. Steam asparagus 4 min. Toss quinoa with cucumber, tomatoes, olive oil, lemon.

### Protein Smoothie Bowl (Thu Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 1 scoop vanilla whey protein, 1 frozen banana, handful spinach, ½ cup almond milk, 15g sliced almonds, ¼ cup mixed berries
- **Prep time:** 5 min
- Blend protein, banana, spinach, almond milk until thick. Pour into bowl, top with almonds and berries.

### Packed Chicken-Rice Box (Thu Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 170g chicken breast (cooked previous night), ½ cup cooked brown rice, 1 cup broccoli florets, 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, ½ tsp sesame oil
- **Prep time:** 10 min assembly (15 min if cooking fresh)
- Stir-fry broccoli 3 min. Pack rice, chicken (sliced), and broccoli. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil before sealing.

### Cottage Cheese Toast (Fri Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 2 slices whole-grain bread (toasted), 150g low-fat cottage cheese, ½ cucumber (sliced thin), 8 cherry tomatoes (halved), dried herbs, black pepper
- **Prep time:** 5 min
- Spread cottage cheese on toast, layer cucumber and tomatoes, season generously.

### Stir-fried Tofu + Brown Rice (Fri Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 200g firm tofu (pressed, cubed), 1 cup broccoli, 1 small carrot (julienned), ½ cup snap peas, ½ cup cooked brown rice, 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, ½ tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water
- **Prep time:** 20 min
- Pan-fry tofu until golden on all sides. Add vegetables, stir-fry 3–4 min. Add soy sauce + cornstarch slurry. Serve over rice.

### Veggie Egg Muffins (Sat Breakfast — makes 6)
- **Ingredients:** 6 large eggs, 1 cup baby spinach (chopped), 6 cherry tomatoes (halved), 30g low-fat shredded cheese, salt, pepper, spray oil
- **Prep time:** 25 min
- Preheat oven 180°C. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper. Spray muffin tin, fill each cup with spinach and tomato, pour egg mixture, top with cheese. Bake 18–20 min until set. Makes 6 muffins; eat 3 now, refrigerate 3 for next morning.

### Poached Salmon Salad (Sat Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150g salmon fillet, 2 cups mixed salad greens, ½ avocado (sliced), ½ cucumber (sliced), 1 tsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, salt, pepper
- **Prep time:** 15 min
- Poach salmon in simmering water with lemon slices for 8–10 min. Flake onto greens with avocado and cucumber. Dress with olive oil and lemon.

### Egg French Toast (Sun Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 2 slices whole-grain bread, 2 large eggs, 1 tsp vanilla extract, ½ tsp cinnamon, spray oil, ½ cup strawberries (sliced)
- **Prep time:** 10 min
- Whisk eggs with vanilla and cinnamon. Soak bread slices 1 min per side. Cook in lightly sprayed pan over medium heat, 2–3 min per side until golden. Serve with fresh strawberries.

### Chicken-Avocado Wrap (Sun Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150g grilled chicken breast (sliced), 1 whole-grain tortilla, ½ avocado (sliced), 2 large lettuce leaves, 4 cherry tomatoes (halved), squeeze of lemon
- **Prep time:** 10 min
- Layer all ingredients on tortilla, season with salt and pepper, squeeze lemon, roll tightly.

### Grilled Chicken + Lentil Soup (Sun Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 180g chicken thighs, 1 cup cooked green/red lentils, ½ cup diced tomatoes (canned), ½ onion (diced), garlic, 1 tsp cumin (non-spicy), low-sodium vegetable stock, mixed roasted vegetables (zucchini, capsicum, carrots)
- **Prep time:** 30 min
- Grill seasoned chicken thighs 6–7 min per side. Sauté onion and garlic, add lentils and tomatoes, simmer in stock 15 min with cumin (mild, not spicy). Roast vegetables at 200°C for 20 min.

## Hydration Reminder
- Aim for **2–2.5L of water** per day
- Green tea or black coffee (no sugar) are good additions — both support fat oxidation
- Avoid fruit juices, bubble tea, and sweetened drinks

## Tips for Success
1. **Batch cook** brown rice, quinoa, and chicken on Sunday night for Mon–Wed packed lunches.
2. **Egg muffins** on Saturday morning — bake 6, eat 3, refrigerate 3 for Sunday breakfast.
3. At restaurants, always **request sauces/dressings on the side** and choose steamed/grilled over fried.
4. Keep **hard-boiled eggs** in the fridge at all times as an emergency protein snack.
5. At Margaret Market and hawker stalls, always **ask for no chilli** when ordering.
