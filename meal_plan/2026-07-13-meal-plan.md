## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Scrambled Eggs on Whole Grain Toast with Spinach
**Serves 1 | Prep: 8 min | ~380 kcal | 26 g protein**
- 3 large eggs, beaten
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
- Salt, pepper to taste
1. Heat oil in non-stick pan over medium heat. Add spinach, wilt 1–2 min, set aside.
2. Pour eggs into pan, stir gently until just set. Season.
3. Serve on toast with spinach on the side.

### Protein Oatmeal
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min | ~380 kcal | 30 g protein**
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 200 ml unsweetened almond milk
- 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
- ½ banana, sliced
- Pinch of cinnamon
1. Cook oats in almond milk (microwave 2–3 min or stovetop).
2. Stir in protein powder off the heat.
3. Top with banana and cinnamon.

### Egg White Omelette with Mushrooms
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min | ~320 kcal | 30 g protein**
- 4 egg whites + 1 whole egg
- 100 g button mushrooms, sliced
- 30 g reduced-fat cheese
- 1 slice whole grain toast
- Cooking spray
1. Spray pan with cooking spray. Sauté mushrooms 3 min.
2. Pour beaten eggs over mushrooms, cook on medium-low until set.
3. Add cheese, fold, serve with toast.

### Tuna Quinoa Salad
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min (+ quinoa cook time) | ~420 kcal | 42 g protein**
- 1 can (185 g) tuna in water, drained
- ½ cup cooked quinoa
- 2 cups mixed greens
- ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Dressing: 1 tsp olive oil + juice of ½ lemon + salt & pepper
1. Combine tuna, quinoa, greens and tomatoes in a bowl.
2. Drizzle dressing and toss.

### Turkey Whole Grain Wrap
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min | ~450 kcal | 35 g protein**
- 1 whole grain tortilla (large)
- 100 g sliced lean turkey breast
- ¼ avocado, sliced
- 2 leaves romaine lettuce
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
1. Lay tortilla flat. Spread mustard.
2. Layer turkey, avocado, lettuce, tomato.
3. Roll tightly and cut in half.

### Pan-Seared Chicken with Brown Rice & Roasted Vegetables
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | ~430 kcal | 45 g protein**
- 200 g chicken breast, flattened
- ½ cup brown rice (cooked)
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 medium carrot, sliced
- 1 tsp olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder
1. Season chicken and sear in oil 4–5 min each side until cooked through. Rest 3 min.
2. Toss vegetables in a little oil, roast at 200 °C for 20 min.
3. Serve chicken over rice with vegetables.

### Baked Salmon with Asparagus and Quinoa
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 20 min | ~460 kcal | 42 g protein**
- 200 g salmon fillet
- 1 bunch asparagus
- ½ cup cooked quinoa
- Juice of ½ lemon, 1 tsp olive oil, dried herbs
1. Place salmon and asparagus on baking tray. Drizzle olive oil and lemon. Season.
2. Bake at 200 °C for 18–20 min.
3. Serve over quinoa.

### Steamed Sea Bass with Bok Choy and Brown Rice
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 15 min | ~380 kcal | 38 g protein**
- 200 g sea bass fillet
- 2 heads baby bok choy, halved
- ½ cup brown rice (cooked)
- 1 clove garlic, minced; 1 tsp light soy sauce; 1 tsp sesame oil
1. Steam sea bass over boiling water 12–15 min.
2. Stir-fry bok choy with garlic 2 min; drizzle light soy and sesame oil.
3. Plate with brown rice.

### Lean Beef Stir-Fry on Cauliflower Rice
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 10 min | ~420 kcal | 40 g protein**
- 175 g lean sirloin, thin strips
- 1½ cups broccoli florets
- ½ bell pepper (any colour), sliced
- 200 g cauliflower, riced (grated or blitzed)
- Sauce: 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tsp grated ginger + 1 tsp sesame oil
1. Pan-fry cauliflower rice in dry pan 3–4 min until tender; set aside.
2. Stir-fry beef on high heat 2–3 min. Add vegetables, cook 3 min.
3. Pour sauce over, toss. Serve on cauliflower rice.

### Salmon Salad with Edamame
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min | ~490 kcal | 46 g protein**
- 150 g grilled salmon (or 1 can pink salmon, drained)
- 1 hard-boiled egg, halved
- 2 cups mixed greens
- ½ cup shelled edamame
- ½ cup cucumber, sliced
- Dressing: 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tbsp low-sodium soy + 1 tsp rice vinegar + ½ tsp grated ginger
1. Arrange greens in bowl. Top with salmon, edamame, cucumber and egg.
2. Drizzle dressing.

### Chicken Rice Bowl
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 20 min | ~450 kcal | 44 g protein**
- 180 g chicken breast, grilled or poached and sliced
- ½ cup brown rice (cooked)
- 1 cup broccoli + ½ cup snap peas, steamed
- 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce drizzle
1. Season and grill chicken breast until cooked through; slice.
2. Steam vegetables 4–5 min.
3. Bowl up rice, top with chicken and veg, drizzle soy.

### Chicken Soup
**Serves 1–2 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | ~360 kcal per serving | 40 g protein**
- 150 g chicken breast, diced
- 1 medium carrot + 2 stalks celery, sliced
- 6 button mushrooms, halved
- ½ onion, diced
- 50 g rice noodles (dry weight)
- 600 ml low-sodium chicken broth
- Salt, white pepper, dash of sesame oil
1. Bring broth to boil. Add onion, carrot, celery; simmer 10 min.
2. Add chicken and mushrooms; simmer 15 min.
3. Add noodles, cook 5 min. Season and finish with sesame oil.

### Overnight Oats
**Serves 1 | Prep: 5 min (night before) | ~330 kcal | 12 g protein**
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 200 ml unsweetened almond milk
- ½ cup blueberries
- Optional: ½ tsp honey
1. Combine oats, chia seeds and almond milk in a jar. Stir well.
2. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
3. Top with blueberries in the morning. Eat cold or microwave 1–2 min.

### Green Smoothie
**Serves 1 | Prep: 3 min | ~370 kcal | 28 g protein**
- 1 scoop protein powder
- 250 ml unsweetened almond milk
- 1 banana
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- Large handful baby spinach
- 4–5 ice cubes
1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.

### Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs
**Serves 1 | Prep: 10 min | ~400 kcal | 20 g protein**
- 2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
- ½ ripe avocado, mashed
- 2 eggs, poached
- ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Salt, black pepper, lemon juice
1. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt and pepper; spread on toast.
2. Poach eggs in simmering water 3–4 min.
3. Place eggs on toast, top with tomatoes, season.

### Greek Yogurt Granola Bowl
**Serves 1 | Prep: 3 min | ~350 kcal | 20 g protein**
- 200 g non-fat Greek yogurt
- ⅓ cup low-sugar granola
- ½ cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries)
1. Spoon yogurt into bowl, top with granola and berries.
