## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Breakfast Recipes

**Overnight Protein Oats** *(Prep: 5 min the night before)*
- 60 g rolled oats + 200 ml low-fat milk + 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- Stir together, refrigerate overnight. Top with ½ sliced banana before eating.

**3-Egg Omelette with Spinach & Mushrooms** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- Beat 3 eggs with salt and pepper. Cook mushrooms (100 g) in a non-stick pan with 1 tsp olive oil until golden.
- Add baby spinach (50 g), pour egg mixture over, fold. Serve with 1 slice toasted whole-grain bread.

**Protein Smoothie** *(Prep: 5 min)*
- Blend: 1 banana + 200 ml low-fat milk + 1 scoop vanilla protein powder + 1 tbsp peanut butter + handful of ice. Drink immediately.

**Greek Yogurt Parfait** *(Prep: 5 min)*
- Layer in a bowl: 200 g low-fat Greek yogurt → 30 g oats → ½ cup mixed berries → 1 tsp honey.

**Avocado Toast & Poached Eggs** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- Toast 1 slice whole-grain bread. Mash ½ avocado with a squeeze of lemon, salt, pepper. Top with 2 poached eggs and halved cherry tomatoes.

**Wholegrain Congee with Steamed Egg** *(Prep: 30 min — use rice cooker)*
- Rinse 80 g wholegrain rice, cook with 600 ml low-sodium chicken stock on congee setting.
- Whisk 2 eggs with 60 ml water, steam for 10 min. Serve congee topped with egg, sliced ginger, spring onion, and a few drops of low-sodium soy sauce.

**Egg White Omelette with Cottage Cheese** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- Whisk 4 egg whites, season with salt and pepper. Cook in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
- Fill with 100 g low-fat cottage cheese and 2 sliced tomatoes, fresh basil. Fold and serve with 1 slice whole-grain toast.

---

### Lunch Recipes

**Tuna Quinoa Salad** *(Prep: 10 min, cook quinoa night before)*
- Base: 80 g cooked quinoa (cook 40 g dry). Add 60 g drained canned tuna, ½ diced cucumber, 10 halved cherry tomatoes, 1 handful rocket.
- Dress with juice of ½ lemon + 1 tsp olive oil + salt + black pepper. Toss and pack.

**Grilled Chicken Rice Box** *(Prep: 20 min)*
- Marinate 130 g chicken breast in low-sodium soy sauce + garlic + ginger for 15 min. Grill or pan-sear until cooked through.
- Slice and pack with 80 g cooked brown rice, 100 g steamed broccoli & carrots, sliced cucumber.

**Smoked Salmon Quinoa Bowl** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- Combine 80 g cooked quinoa, 100 g smoked salmon (sliced), 80 g thawed edamame, diced cucumber.
- Dress with 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + ½ tsp rice vinegar. Top with sesame seeds.

**Teriyaki Chicken Bowl** *(Prep: 20 min)*
- Sauce: 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tsp honey + 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water.
- Pan-cook 130 g sliced chicken breast, pour sauce in last 2 min to glaze. Serve over 80 g cooked brown rice with steamed broccoli & edamame.

---

### Dinner Recipes

**Baked Lemon-Herb Salmon** *(Prep: 25 min)*
- Preheat oven 200 °C. Place 150 g salmon fillet on foil-lined tray. Drizzle with 1 tsp olive oil, squeeze of lemon, dried herbs (dill/thyme), salt, pepper.
- Bake 15–18 min. Serve with 100 g cooked brown rice and 200 g steamed broccoli.

**Stir-Fried Chicken & Bok Choy** *(Prep: 20 min)*
- Heat 1 tsp oil in wok. Stir-fry 3 cloves minced garlic 30 sec. Add 150 g chicken breast strips, cook through (~5 min).
- Add 300 g bok choy (halved), stir-fry 3 min. Season with 1 tbsp low-sodium oyster sauce + splash of water. Serve with 100 g cooked brown rice.

**Steamed Seabass with Ginger-Soy** *(Prep: 25 min)*
- Place 150 g seabass fillet on plate, top with julienned ginger and 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tsp sesame oil. Steam over boiling water 10–12 min.
- Serve with 150 g baked sweet potato (pierce, microwave 5 min or bake 25 min) and 200 g kai lan stir-fried with garlic in ½ tsp oil.

## Meal Prep Tips

1. **Sunday prep session (~60 min):** Cook a big batch of brown rice and quinoa. Grill 2–3 chicken breasts. Hard-boil 6 eggs. Wash and portion vegetables.
2. **Overnight oats:** Make Monday's and Wednesday's oats the night before — takes 5 min.
3. **Lunchboxes:** Pack Tuesday and Thursday lunchboxes the night before using prepped ingredients.
4. **Restaurant orders:** Always ask for sauces/dressings on the side, choose steamed over fried, and request extra vegetables instead of extra rice.
