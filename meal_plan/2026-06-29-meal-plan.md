## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Breakfast Recipes

**Smoked Salmon & Cottage Cheese Toast** *(Prep: 5 min)*
- 2 slices wholegrain bread (toasted), 80 g smoked salmon, 100 g low-fat cottage cheese, ¼ small red onion (thin rings), 1 tsp capers, squeeze of lemon, black pepper
- Spread cottage cheese on toasted slices. Layer smoked salmon. Top with onion, capers and lemon. Season with pepper.

**3-Egg Spinach & Mushroom Omelette** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- 3 eggs (beaten, seasoned with salt & pepper), 100 g sliced mushrooms, 50 g baby spinach, 1 slice wholegrain toast, 1 tsp olive oil
- Heat oil in non-stick pan. Sauté mushrooms 2 min until golden. Add spinach, wilt 1 min. Pour beaten eggs over, cook until set, fold. Serve with toast.

**Greek Yogurt Parfait** *(Prep: 5 min)*
- 200 g low-fat Greek yogurt, 30 g rolled oats, ½ cup mixed berries, 1 tsp honey
- Layer in a bowl: yogurt → oats → berries → honey drizzle. Eat immediately or refrigerate overnight (oats soften nicely).

**Smoothie Bowl** *(Prep: 5 min)*
- 100 g frozen mixed berries, ½ banana (sliced), 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, 150 ml unsweetened almond milk, 25 g low-sugar granola (topping)
- Blend berries, banana, protein powder and almond milk until thick (use as little liquid as possible). Pour into bowl, top with granola. Eat with a spoon.

**Protein Pancakes** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- 2 large eggs, 1 scoop whey protein (any flavour), ½ ripe banana (mashed), 2 tbsp unsweetened almond milk, 100 g sliced strawberries
- Whisk eggs, protein, mashed banana and almond milk into smooth batter. Cook 2 pancakes on non-stick pan over medium-low heat, ~2 min per side. Serve topped with strawberries.

**Avocado Toast & Poached Eggs** *(Prep: 10 min)*
- 2 slices wholegrain bread (toasted), ½ avocado, 2 eggs, 6 cherry tomatoes (halved), ½ lemon, salt, black pepper
- Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Spread on toast. Poach eggs in simmering water with a splash of vinegar, 3–4 min. Top toast with eggs and cherry tomatoes.

**Cottage Cheese & Crispbread Platter** *(Prep: 3 min)*
- 200 g low-fat cottage cheese, 2 wholegrain crispbreads (Ryvita), ½ cucumber (sliced), 2 tomatoes (sliced), dried herbs (Italian mix or dill), black pepper
- Arrange on a plate. Season cottage cheese with herbs and pepper. A quick, no-cook breakfast.

### Lunch Recipes

**Tuna & Edamame Grain Bowl** *(Prep: 10 min; cook quinoa the night before)*
- 80 g cooked quinoa, 120 g canned tuna in water (drained), 60 g shelled edamame (thawed from frozen), ½ cucumber (diced), 8 cherry tomatoes (halved)
- Dressing: juice of ½ lemon + 1 tsp olive oil + salt + black pepper
- Toss all ingredients with dressing. Pack in a container for the office.

**Baked Salmon Quinoa Bowl** *(Prep: 25 min)*
- 150 g salmon fillet, 80 g cooked quinoa, 80 g shelled edamame (thawed), ½ cucumber (diced), 8 cherry tomatoes (halved)
- Dressing: 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + ½ tsp rice vinegar
- Season salmon with salt and pepper. Bake at 200 °C for 15 min. Flake and place over quinoa, edamame, cucumber and tomatoes. Drizzle dressing.

**Big Tuna Salad Bowl** *(Prep: 8 min)*
- 120 g canned tuna in water (drained), 2 hard-boiled eggs (halved), 80 g mixed salad greens, 10 cherry tomatoes, ½ cucumber (sliced), 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt & pepper
- Combine all ingredients. Toss with olive oil and lemon. Serve immediately.

### Dinner Recipes

**Baked Lemon Herb Cod** *(Prep: 25 min)*
- 150 g cod fillet, 1 tsp olive oil, lemon slices, pinch of dried thyme & dill, salt, pepper
- 150 g sweet potato (cubed and roasted with ½ tsp olive oil, 20 min at 200 °C)
- 200 g kai lan (stir-fried with 1 garlic clove + ½ tsp oil, 3 min, splash of water)
- Preheat oven to 200 °C. Place cod on foil-lined tray, drizzle with oil, top with lemon slices and herbs. Bake 12–15 min alongside sweet potato.

**Turkey & Veggie Rice Bowl** *(Prep: 20 min)*
- 120 g lean ground turkey, 100 g broccoli + 60 g snap peas + 60 g carrots (bite-size pieces), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tbsp low-sodium oyster sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil, 80 g cooked brown rice
- Brown turkey in pan over medium-high heat, break up with spatula (~5 min). Push aside, add garlic and vegetables, stir-fry 3 min. Combine, add oyster sauce and sesame oil, toss well. Serve over rice.

**Pan-Seared Chicken Thigh with Asparagus & Quinoa** *(Prep: 25 min)*
- 150 g skinless boneless chicken thigh, 150 g asparagus (trimmed), 80 g cooked quinoa, 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, salt, pepper, lemon wedge
- Preheat oven to 200 °C. Season chicken with garlic powder, salt, pepper. Sear in oven-proof pan with olive oil, 4 min per side. Transfer to oven along with asparagus (drizzled with oil) for 10 min. Serve with quinoa and lemon wedge.

**Stir-Fried Beef with Broccoli & Brown Rice** *(Prep: 20 min)*
- 120 g lean beef (sirloin or flank, thinly sliced), 200 g broccoli florets, 80 g snap peas, 1 tbsp low-sodium oyster sauce, 1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tsp cornstarch (dissolved in 2 tbsp water), 80 g cooked brown rice
- Toss beef slices in cornstarch slurry. Heat sesame oil in wok until smoking. Stir-fry garlic 30 sec. Add beef, cook 2 min until browned. Add broccoli and snap peas, stir-fry 3 min. Pour in oyster sauce and soy sauce, toss to coat. Serve over rice.

## Meal Prep Tips

1. **Sunday prep session (~60 min):** Cook a big batch of brown rice and quinoa. Hard-boil 6–8 eggs. Wash and chop broccoli, snap peas, carrots. Thaw edamame in the fridge.
2. **Overnight prep:** Wed grain bowl can be assembled the night before (keep dressing separate). Thu quinoa can be cooked Tuesday night.
3. **Lunchboxes:** Wed and Sun lunches are fully self-made — prep the night before to avoid morning rush.
4. **Hawker centre ordering tips:** Request steamed rice instead of oil-soaked; ask for sauces on the side; add a plate of steamed greens (chye sim or kai lan) for extra fibre and volume; always skip chilli.
5. **SaladStop! ordering tip:** Use the in-store nutritional guide or app to pre-select your bowl and track calories before you queue.
