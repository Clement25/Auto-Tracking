## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Scrambled Eggs + Toast + Spinach (Mon Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 3 eggs, 2 slices whole grain bread, 100 g baby spinach, olive oil spray, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Sauté spinach in sprayed pan (2 min). Scramble eggs in same pan. Toast bread. Serve together.
- **Prep time:** 8 minutes | **Calories:** ~430 kcal | **Protein:** 28 g

### Overnight Oats (Tue Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 50 g rolled oats, 200 ml low-fat milk, 1 tsp chia seeds, handful blueberries
- **Method:** Combine oats, milk, and chia seeds in a jar. Refrigerate overnight. Top with berries in the morning.
- **Prep time:** 3 minutes (night before) | **Calories:** ~380 kcal | **Protein:** 18 g

### Almond Butter Oatmeal (Wed Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 60 g rolled oats, 300 ml water, 1 tbsp almond butter, 1 banana (sliced)
- **Method:** Cook oats with water 3–4 min. Stir in almond butter. Top with banana slices.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes | **Calories:** ~400 kcal | **Protein:** 14 g

### Protein Smoothie Bowl (Thu Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 1 banana, 150 g frozen mixed berries, 1 scoop protein powder (vanilla), 200 ml unsweetened almond milk
- **Method:** Blend all ingredients. Pour into bowl. Garnish with a few berries.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes | **Calories:** ~350 kcal | **Protein:** 28 g

### Boiled Eggs with Avocado & Rye (Fri Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 3 eggs, 1 slice rye bread, 50 g avocado, lemon juice, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Boil eggs 7 min. Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon, salt. Top toast with avocado, eggs on side.
- **Prep time:** 10 minutes | **Calories:** ~420 kcal | **Protein:** 24 g

### Protein Pancakes (Sat Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, 1 banana, 2 eggs, 30 g rolled oats
- **Method:** Blend all ingredients. Cook small pancakes in non-stick pan (no oil) on medium heat, 2–3 min per side.
- **Prep time:** 12 minutes | **Calories:** ~450 kcal | **Protein:** 35 g

### Egg White Omelette (Sun Breakfast)
- **Ingredients:** 4 egg whites, 1 whole egg, 80 g button mushrooms (sliced), 1 tomato (diced), 1 slice whole grain toast, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Whisk eggs. Cook mushrooms 2 min. Add egg mixture, fold omelette. Serve with toast.
- **Prep time:** 10 minutes | **Calories:** ~320 kcal | **Protein:** 24 g

### Tuna Whole-Grain Wrap (Wed Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 120 g canned tuna (in water, drained), 1 whole grain tortilla wrap, 50 g mixed greens, ½ cucumber (sliced), 1 tsp light mayo, lemon juice
- **Method:** Mix tuna with mayo and lemon. Layer greens and cucumber on wrap. Add tuna, fold and pack.
- **Prep time:** 7 minutes | **Calories:** ~420 kcal | **Protein:** 35 g

### Stir-fried Chicken + Vegetables + Brown Rice (Tue Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 160 g chicken breast, 150 g bok choy, 100 g mushrooms, 80 g brown rice (uncooked ~200 g cooked), 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, garlic, ginger
- **Method:** Cook rice. Stir-fry garlic and ginger, add chicken (sliced) until cooked. Add vegetables, soy sauce, stir 3 min.
- **Prep time:** 20 minutes | **Calories:** ~500 kcal | **Protein:** 42 g

### Baked Salmon + Broccoli + Sweet Potato (Mon Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 180 g salmon fillet, 200 g broccoli florets, 150 g sweet potato (cubed), olive oil, lemon, garlic, rosemary, salt
- **Method:** Pre-heat oven 200°C. Toss broccoli and sweet potato with olive oil, season. Roast 20 min. Season salmon, bake 15 min.
- **Prep time:** 30 minutes | **Calories:** ~530 kcal | **Protein:** 40 g

### Soba Noodles with Tofu (Thu Dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 80 g soba noodles (dry), 100 g firm tofu (cubed), 100 g frozen edamame, 100 g bok choy, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, ½ tsp rice vinegar
- **Method:** Cook noodles per packet (4–5 min). Steam bok choy. Mix dressing. Combine all ingredients.
- **Prep time:** 15 minutes | **Calories:** ~480 kcal | **Protein:** 25 g

### Grilled Chicken + Quinoa + Vegetables (Sat Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 180 g chicken breast, 80 g quinoa (uncooked), 100 g broccoli, 80 g carrot (sliced), olive oil spray, lemon, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Cook quinoa per packet (12 min). Steam vegetables. Season and grill chicken in pan (6–7 min per side). Slice and serve over quinoa with vegetables.
- **Prep time:** 25 minutes | **Calories:** ~550 kcal | **Protein:** 48 g

### Tuna Brown Rice Bowl (Sun Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 120 g canned tuna (in water, drained), 100 g cooked brown rice, ½ cucumber (diced), 30 g avocado (sliced), 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, lemon juice
- **Method:** Layer rice in bowl. Top with cucumber and avocado. Add tuna, drizzle soy-lemon dressing.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes (if rice pre-cooked) | **Calories:** ~480 kcal | **Protein:** 40 g
