## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Breakfast Recipes

#### Greek Yogurt Parfait (Mon)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g low-fat Greek yogurt, 1 scoop vanilla whey protein, 50 g frozen mixed berries (thawed), 30 g low-sugar granola
- **Method:** Layer yogurt + protein mix in a bowl or jar, top with berries and granola. Mix well before eating.
- **Prep time:** 3 minutes
- **Macros:** ~360 kcal | P: 32 g | C: 38 g | F: 8 g

#### Overnight Oats (Tue)
- **Ingredients:** 50 g rolled oats, 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, 150 ml low-fat milk, 80 g strawberries, pinch of cinnamon
- **Method:** Mix oats, protein, and milk the night before. Refrigerate overnight. Top with sliced strawberries in the morning.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes (night before)
- **Macros:** ~380 kcal | P: 28 g | C: 48 g | F: 8 g

#### Egg-White Omelette (Wed)
- **Ingredients:** 2 whole eggs, 2 egg whites, handful baby spinach, ½ tomato (diced), salt & pepper, 1 slice wholegrain bread
- **Method:** Whisk eggs and egg whites. Pour into non-stick pan on medium heat. Add spinach and tomato, fold omelette. Serve with toasted wholegrain bread.
- **Prep time:** 8 minutes
- **Macros:** ~350 kcal | P: 28 g | C: 22 g | F: 14 g

#### Protein Banana Smoothie (Thu)
- **Ingredients:** 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, 1 ripe banana, 150 ml low-fat milk, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 4–5 ice cubes
- **Method:** Blend all ingredients until smooth.
- **Prep time:** 3 minutes
- **Macros:** ~390 kcal | P: 32 g | C: 42 g | F: 10 g

#### Avocado Toast with Scrambled Eggs (Fri)
- **Ingredients:** 2 slices wholegrain bread, 2 eggs, ¼ ripe avocado, 1 tomato (sliced), salt, pepper, lemon juice
- **Method:** Toast bread. Scramble eggs in a non-stick pan. Mash avocado with lemon juice and spread on toast. Top with eggs and tomato.
- **Prep time:** 8 minutes
- **Macros:** ~430 kcal | P: 24 g | C: 36 g | F: 18 g

#### High-Protein Banana Pancakes (Sat)
- **Ingredients:** 1 ripe banana, 2 eggs, 1 scoop protein powder, 50 g mixed berries (for topping)
- **Method:** Mash banana, mix in eggs and protein powder to form a batter. Cook small pancakes on a non-stick pan over medium-low heat (~2 min each side). Top with berries.
- **Prep time:** 12 minutes
- **Macros:** ~420 kcal | P: 34 g | C: 38 g | F: 12 g

#### Baked Egg Muffins (Sun)
- **Ingredients:** 4 eggs, ½ bell pepper (diced), handful baby spinach, 4 mushrooms (sliced), salt, pepper, optional low-fat cheese
- **Method:** Preheat oven to 180 °C. Grease muffin tin. Divide vegetables evenly into 4 cups. Whisk eggs, season, pour over vegetables. Bake 18–20 min until set. Serve with toasted wholegrain bread.
- **Prep time:** 25 minutes
- **Macros:** ~380 kcal | P: 28 g | C: 20 g | F: 18 g

### Lunch & Dinner Recipes

#### Baked Salmon with Brown Rice & Steamed Vegetables (Mon dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g salmon fillet, 100 g dry brown rice (cook per pack), 150 g broccoli florets, 1 medium carrot (sliced), 1 tsp olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Preheat oven to 200 °C. Drizzle salmon with olive oil, season, bake 15–18 min. Steam broccoli and carrots 5–7 min. Serve over cooked brown rice.
- **Prep time:** 25 minutes
- **Macros:** ~500 kcal | P: 42 g | C: 40 g | F: 16 g

#### Tuna Salad Wrap (Wed lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 1 large wholegrain tortilla, 120 g canned tuna in water (drained), handful shredded lettuce, ½ cucumber (sliced), 8 cherry tomatoes (halved), 1 tsp low-fat mayo, squeeze of lemon, black pepper
- **Method:** Mix tuna with mayo, lemon, and pepper. Layer lettuce, tuna mix, cucumber, and tomatoes on tortilla. Roll tightly and slice in half.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes
- **Macros:** ~420 kcal | P: 40 g | C: 36 g | F: 10 g

#### Chicken & Quinoa Bowl (Thu lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g chicken breast, 80 g dry quinoa (cook per pack, yields ~200 g), 80 g mixed salad greens, 8 cherry tomatoes, ½ cucumber, 1 tsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Season and grill/pan-fry chicken breast until cooked (10–12 min). Slice and place on quinoa. Top with greens, tomatoes, cucumber. Drizzle olive oil and lemon.
- **Prep time:** 20 minutes
- **Macros:** ~480 kcal | P: 42 g | C: 38 g | F: 14 g

#### Lean Beef Stir-Fry with Brown Rice (Fri dinner)
- **Ingredients:** 120 g lean beef strips (sirloin or topside), 100 g cooked brown rice, 100 g broccoli, ½ red bell pepper (sliced), 50 g snap peas, 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp grated ginger, 1 garlic clove (minced)
- **Method:** Heat wok on high. Stir-fry beef 2–3 min until browned, set aside. Stir-fry vegetables 3–4 min. Return beef, add soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic. Toss and serve over brown rice.
- **Prep time:** 20 minutes
- **Macros:** ~480 kcal | P: 38 g | C: 48 g | F: 12 g

#### Salmon Salad Bowl (Sat lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 120 g canned salmon (drained), 80 g mixed greens, 8 cherry tomatoes (halved), ½ cucumber (sliced), 1 hard-boiled egg (quartered), 1 tsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, salt, pepper
- **Method:** Arrange greens in a bowl. Top with salmon, tomatoes, cucumber, and egg. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon. Season.
- **Prep time:** 5 minutes
- **Macros:** ~400 kcal | P: 40 g | C: 8 g | F: 22 g

#### Chicken & Vegetable Soup (Sun lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g chicken breast (diced), 1 medium carrot (diced), 1 stalk celery (sliced), ½ onion (diced), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 500 ml low-sodium chicken stock, salt, pepper, parsley; serve with 80 g cooked brown rice on the side
- **Method:** Sauté onion and garlic in a pot 2 min. Add carrots, celery, and stock. Bring to boil, add chicken, simmer 15–18 min until chicken is cooked through. Season. Serve with brown rice.
- **Prep time:** 25 minutes
- **Macros:** ~450 kcal | P: 40 g | C: 44 g | F: 8 g
