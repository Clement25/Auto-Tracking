## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### Breakfast Recipes

#### B1 — Scrambled Eggs on Whole-Grain Toast with Avocado
- **Ingredients:** 2 large eggs, 1 slice whole-grain bread, ½ ripe avocado, salt & pepper, olive oil spray
- **Method:** Whisk eggs, cook in a non-stick pan on low heat with olive oil spray, stirring slowly. Toast bread. Slice avocado and season with a pinch of salt.
- **Prep time:** 10 min | **~380 kcal | 22 g protein**

#### B2 — Overnight Oats with Protein Powder
- **Ingredients:** 40 g rolled oats, 200 ml unsweetened almond milk, 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (~25 g protein), 80 g mixed berries (fresh or frozen), 1 tsp chia seeds
- **Method:** Mix oats, almond milk, protein powder, and chia seeds in a jar the night before. Refrigerate. Top with berries in the morning.
- **Prep time:** 5 min (night before) | **~370 kcal | 32 g protein**

#### B3 — Spinach & Mushroom Omelette
- **Ingredients:** 3 large eggs, 80 g baby spinach, 100 g mushrooms (sliced), 1 slice whole-grain toast, olive oil spray, salt & pepper
- **Method:** Sauté mushrooms 3 min, add spinach until wilted. Pour whisked eggs over, fold when set. Serve with toast.
- **Prep time:** 12 min | **~360 kcal | 26 g protein**

#### B4 — Banana Protein Smoothie
- **Ingredients:** 1 ripe banana, 1 scoop protein powder, 200 ml low-fat milk, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 1 handful baby spinach, 3–4 ice cubes
- **Method:** Blend all ingredients until smooth.
- **Prep time:** 5 min | **~400 kcal | 30 g protein**

#### B5 — Greek Yogurt Chia Bowl + Boiled Eggs
- **Ingredients:** 150 g low-fat Greek yogurt, 80 g mixed berries, 2 tbsp chia seeds, 2 boiled eggs
- **Method:** Mix yogurt and chia seeds, top with berries. Boil eggs 9 min for hard-boiled.
- **Prep time:** 5 min (+ egg boiling) | **~430 kcal | 30 g protein**

#### B6 — Boiled Eggs with Smoked Salmon Toast
- **Ingredients:** 2 large eggs, 1 slice whole-grain bread, 30 g smoked salmon, a few slices of cucumber, black pepper
- **Method:** Hard-boil eggs, toast bread, layer smoked salmon and cucumber on toast.
- **Prep time:** 10 min | **~340 kcal | 28 g protein**

#### B7 — Cantonese Chicken Congee
- **Ingredients:** 100 g jasmine or long-grain rice, 80 g chicken breast (shredded), 5 g fresh ginger (sliced), 2 spring onion stalks, 800 ml chicken stock (low-sodium), soy sauce (light, 1 tsp)
- **Method:** Bring stock and ginger to a boil, add rice, simmer 35–40 min (stirring occasionally) until porridge consistency. Stir in shredded chicken (pre-cooked or raw, cook through). Garnish with spring onion.
- **Prep time:** 40 min (or overnight in slow cooker) | **~350 kcal | 24 g protein**

### Lunch / Dinner Recipes

#### D1 — Pan-Seared Salmon with Brown Rice & Broccoli
- **Ingredients:** 150 g salmon fillet, 100 g cooked brown rice, 200 g broccoli florets, olive oil (1 tsp), salt, lemon juice
- **Method:** Season salmon with salt and lemon. Sear skin-side down in a hot pan with olive oil for 4 min, flip for 2 min. Steam broccoli 5 min. Serve with pre-cooked brown rice.
- **Prep time:** 20 min | **~520 kcal | 42 g protein**

#### D2 — Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato & Bok Choy
- **Ingredients:** 150 g chicken breast, 120 g sweet potato (cubed), 200 g bok choy, olive oil (1 tsp), garlic (1 clove), soy sauce (1 tsp)
- **Method:** Marinate chicken in soy sauce and garlic 15 min, grill or pan-cook 6–7 min each side. Roast sweet potato at 200 °C for 20 min. Stir-fry bok choy with garlic 3 min.
- **Prep time:** 25 min | **~450 kcal | 42 g protein**

#### D3 — Steamed Barramundi with Quinoa & Asparagus
- **Ingredients:** 150 g barramundi fillet, 80 g dry quinoa (cook per packet), 200 g asparagus, ginger (5 g sliced), 1 tsp light soy sauce, sesame oil (few drops)
- **Method:** Place barramundi on a plate with ginger, steam over boiling water 8–10 min. Drizzle with soy sauce and a few drops sesame oil. Cook quinoa. Roast asparagus at 200 °C for 12 min.
- **Prep time:** 25 min | **~490 kcal | 40 g protein**

#### D4 — Turkey Meatballs with Zucchini Noodles
- **Ingredients:** 150 g lean turkey mince, 1 egg white, 1 clove garlic (minced), Italian herbs, 400 g canned crushed tomatoes, 2 medium zucchini (spiralised or julienned)
- **Method:** Mix turkey, egg white, garlic, and herbs; roll into 8–10 balls; bake at 200 °C for 18 min. Simmer tomato sauce in a pan 10 min. Sauté zucchini noodles 2 min. Plate noodles, top with sauce and meatballs.
- **Prep time:** 30 min | **~450 kcal | 40 g protein**

#### SL1 — Meal-Prep Chicken & Brown Rice Lunch Bowl (Friday)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g chicken breast (grilled the night before), 100 g cooked brown rice, 80 g frozen edamame (thawed), 1 small cucumber (sliced), 1 tbsp sesame dressing (store-bought low-cal), sesame seeds (optional)
- **Method:** Grill chicken Thursday night, slice. Layer rice, chicken, edamame, cucumber in a lunch box. Drizzle dressing just before eating.
- **Prep time:** 10 min (pack-up) | **~480 kcal | 40 g protein**

#### SL2 — Tuna Avocado Salad (Saturday Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g canned tuna in water (drained), ½ avocado (cubed), 60 g mixed greens, 80 g cherry tomatoes (halved), ½ lemon (juice), 1 tsp olive oil, salt & pepper
- **Method:** Combine all ingredients, toss gently, season.
- **Prep time:** 10 min | **~400 kcal | 35 g protein**

#### SL3 — Teriyaki Salmon Rice Bowl (Sunday Lunch)
- **Ingredients:** 150 g salmon fillet, 100 g cooked brown rice, 80 g edamame (thawed), 1 small cucumber (sliced), 2 tbsp light teriyaki sauce
- **Method:** Bake salmon at 200 °C for 12–14 min, brush with 1 tbsp teriyaki sauce halfway. Assemble bowl with rice, edamame, cucumber, and salmon. Drizzle remaining sauce.
- **Prep time:** 20 min | **~520 kcal | 42 g protein**

## Customisation Tips for Restaurant Meals

| Restaurant | Tip |
|------------|-----|
| **YOLO (Star Vista)** | Choose cauliflower rice or a mix of cauliflower + brown rice for lower carbs; opt for grilled chicken or salmon over beef; avoid creamy dressings — pick vinaigrette |
| **Supergreen (Star Vista)** | Build-your-own: brown rice or lettuce base, baked salmon or teriyaki chicken, load up on cold toppings (edamame, cucumber, cherry tomatoes), use a light dressing |
| **Cedele Kitchen (Star Vista)** | Ask for grilled protein (not fried); request dressing on the side; choose grain bowl over sandwich for better macro balance |
| **Simply Sandwich (The Metropolis)** | Ask for wholemeal wrap; skip mayo and creamy spreads; add extra salad; choose grilled chicken over deli meat |
| **Margaret Drive Hawker Centre — Fish Soup** | Ask for extra tofu; request less salt in the broth; add a small portion of rice on the side for sustained energy |
| **Margaret Drive Hawker Centre — Chicken Rice** | Request roasted (not poached skin-on) chicken; ask for less rice; get extra steamed vegetables; avoid chilli dips to keep it non-spicy |
| **Margaret Drive Hawker Centre — Thunder Tea Rice** | Confirm they use brown rice if available; ask for extra portions of blanched greens; the savoury tea is nutritious — drink it all |
