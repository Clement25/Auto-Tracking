## Self-Made Meal Recipes

### 1. Greek Yogurt Power Bowl *(Mon Breakfast — ~310 kcal, 24 g P)*
- 200 g low-fat Greek yogurt
- 100 g mixed berries (fresh or frozen, thawed)
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 10 g walnuts, roughly chopped

**Method:** Layer yogurt in a bowl, top with berries, scatter chia seeds and walnuts. Eat immediately.
**Prep time:** 5 min

---

### 2. Pan-Seared Salmon + Brown Rice + Broccoli *(Mon Dinner — ~520 kcal, 40 g P)*
- 150 g salmon fillet
- 120 g cooked brown rice
- 200 g broccoli florets
- 1 tsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, 1 garlic clove (minced), salt, black pepper

**Method:** Season salmon with lemon, garlic, salt, pepper. Heat oil in non-stick pan on medium-high; sear salmon 3–4 min per side. Steam broccoli 5 min. Serve over rice.
**Prep + cook time:** 25 min

---

### 3. Scrambled Eggs with Veggies on Toast *(Tue Breakfast — ~370 kcal, 24 g P)*
- 3 large eggs
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 100 g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 slice whole-wheat bread (toasted)
- Salt, black pepper

**Method:** Sauté mushrooms in oil for 3 min. Add spinach, wilt for 1 min. Pour in beaten eggs; stir gently on low heat until just set. Season and serve on toast.
**Prep + cook time:** 15 min

---

### 4. Overnight Oats *(Wed Breakfast — ~390 kcal, 16 g P)*
- 50 g rolled oats
- 200 ml low-fat milk
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 80 g banana, sliced
- 1 tsp honey

**Method:** Combine oats, milk, and chia seeds in a jar. Stir, seal, refrigerate overnight. In the morning, top with banana and drizzle honey.
**Prep time:** 5 min (night before)

---

### 5. Tofu & Vegetable Stir-Fry + Brown Rice *(Wed Dinner — ~480 kcal, 28 g P)*
- 250 g firm tofu, cubed and patted dry
- 150 g mixed vegetables: bok choy, bell pepper, carrot
- 120 g cooked brown rice
- 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove (minced), ½ tsp ginger (grated)

**Method:** Pan-fry tofu cubes in a non-stick pan until golden (~5 min). Remove. In same pan, stir-fry garlic and ginger 30 sec. Add vegetables, stir-fry 3 min. Return tofu, add soy sauce and sesame oil, toss. Serve over rice.
**Prep + cook time:** 20 min

---

### 6. 3-Egg Vegetable Omelette *(Thu Breakfast — ~380 kcal, 28 g P)*
- 3 large eggs
- 5 cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1 tbsp grated low-fat cheddar
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 slice whole-wheat bread (toasted)
- Salt, black pepper

**Method:** Beat eggs, season. Heat oil in non-stick pan; pour in eggs. When edges set, add tomatoes, spinach, and cheese to one half. Fold over and cook 1 min more. Serve with toast.
**Prep + cook time:** 15 min

---

### 7. Protein Smoothie Bowl *(Fri Breakfast — ~400 kcal, 35 g P)*
- 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder (~25 g)
- 200 ml low-fat milk
- 80 g frozen berries
- 30 g rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds

**Method:** Blend whey, milk, and frozen berries until thick and smooth. Pour into bowl; top with oats and chia seeds.
**Prep time:** 10 min

---

### 8. Herb-Baked Chicken + Roasted Veg + Sweet Potato *(Fri Dinner — ~490 kcal, 46 g P)*
- 180 g chicken breast
- 200 g zucchini + bell pepper, chunked
- 120 g sweet potato, cubed
- 1 tsp olive oil, mixed herbs (oregano, thyme, rosemary), garlic, salt, pepper

**Method:** Preheat oven to 200 °C. Coat chicken and vegetables in oil and herbs; season. Arrange on baking tray (chicken separate). Roast sweet potato 20 min, add chicken and veg, roast further 20–25 min until chicken is cooked through.
**Prep + cook time:** 35 min

---

### 9. Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs *(Sat Breakfast — ~420 kcal, 22 g P)*
- 2 slices whole-wheat bread, toasted
- ½ ripe avocado, mashed
- 2 large eggs (poached)
- 8 cherry tomatoes, halved
- Black pepper, a squeeze of lemon

**Method:** Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon, season. Poach eggs in simmering water 3–4 min. Spread avocado on toast, top with eggs and tomatoes, season with pepper.
**Prep + cook time:** 15 min

---

### 10. Tuna Salad Wrap *(Sat Lunch — ~420 kcal, 44 g P)*
- 150 g canned tuna in water (drained)
- 1 whole-wheat tortilla
- 2 large romaine lettuce leaves
- 5 cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ cucumber, sliced
- 2 tbsp low-fat Greek yogurt (as dressing)
- Squeeze of lemon, black pepper

**Method:** Mix tuna with Greek yogurt, lemon, and pepper. Lay lettuce on tortilla, add tuna mix, tomatoes, and cucumber. Wrap tightly.
**Prep time:** 10 min

---

### 11. Oat Porridge with Greek Yogurt *(Sun Breakfast — ~380 kcal, 24 g P)*
- 50 g rolled oats
- 200 ml low-fat milk
- 100 g low-fat Greek yogurt
- 80 g blueberries
- Pinch of cinnamon

**Method:** Cook oats with milk on medium heat, stirring, ~5 min until thick. Pour into bowl, swirl in yogurt, top with blueberries and cinnamon.
**Cook time:** 10 min

---

### 12. Grilled Chicken Salad with Pitta *(Sun Lunch — ~480 kcal, 47 g P)*
- 150 g chicken breast (grilled or pan-seared)
- 2 large handfuls mixed salad greens
- 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ cucumber, sliced
- 1 hard-boiled egg, halved
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 small whole-wheat pitta
- Salt, black pepper

**Method:** Slice grilled chicken. Toss greens, tomatoes, cucumber with balsamic. Top with chicken and egg. Serve with warmed pitta.
**Prep + cook time:** 20 min

## Restaurant Ordering Tips

| Restaurant | Customisation to Request |
|-----------|------------------------|
| **SaladStop!** | Choose grain bowl base; select grilled (not crispy) protein; light dressing on the side; skip croutons |
| **The Soup Spoon Union** | Order wholegrain roll; ask for a low-sodium soup; skip creamy/chowder options |
| **Dosirak** | Explicitly request **no chilli paste / bibimbap sauce**; ask for light sesame dressing |

| **Hakka Thunder Tea (Lei Cha)** | Ask for brown rice if available; go light on peanuts to manage fat intake |
| **Kou Kou Xiang Chicken Soup** | Request clear broth (not thickened); ask for lean chicken pieces; brown rice if available |
