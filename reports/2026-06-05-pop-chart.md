## Highlights

### US

Drake is dominating the Billboard Hot 100 this week, landing "Janice STFU" at #1 for a second consecutive week — his first multi-week chart-topper since "In My Feelings." The track debuted with a chart-record 42 simultaneous entries and secured Drake's 14th career Hot 100 #1, breaking him out of a tie with Michael Jackson for the most by any solo male artist in the chart's 67-year history. Billboard's Songs of the Summer chart has also returned for 2026 with "Janice STFU" leading the pack. Ella Langley continues to hold strong with both "Choosin' Texas" (#2, the longest-running Hot 100 #1 by a female country artist ever, at 10 non-consecutive weeks) and "Be Her" (#3), making her only the 20th act ever to hold Nos. 1 and 2 simultaneously. Olivia Rodrigo scores a high-profile debut at #5 with "The Cure."

### EU

The UK chart story of 2026 belongs to Sam Fender and Olivia Dean's "Rein Me In," which has now logged 11 non-consecutive weeks at #1 on the Official UK Singles Chart — a new all-time record for a male/female duo, surpassing Rihanna and Jay-Z's "Umbrella." The song, which won the 2026 Brit Award for Song of the Year, continues to dominate Spotify UK streams. Olivia Dean has a remarkable dual presence: she co-owns the #1 record with Fender while her solo single "Man I Need" holds at #3. Olivia Rodrigo's "the cure" sits firmly at #2, and Ariana Grande's emotionally-driven "hate that i made you love me" rounds out the top 4. Drake's US chart invasion bleeds into the UK top 5 as well.

### CN

Zhou Shen's 《奔腾》 (Boundless) remains the most played Mandarin pop song on Douyin in 2026, accumulating over 800 million platform plays and 30 million video likes — fueled by its origins as People's Daily's flagship New Year anthem and its sweeping, anthemic production. Andy Lau's 《非凡》 (Extraordinary) debuted at the top of the QQ Music hot chart, riding his enduring star power and the momentum of his ongoing 2026 concert tour. The emotionally charged duet 《爱人啊》 by actor-singer Jerry Yan and Mayday's Ashin has surpassed 600 million Douyin plays, blending C-pop balladry with nostalgic appeal. Li Ronghao's 《恋人》 (Lover) and Bai Xiaobai's 《人生路漫漫》 (Long Road of Life) round out the top 5, with both tracks sustaining high engagement across QQ Music and NetEase Cloud Music.
