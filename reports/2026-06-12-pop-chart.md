## Highlights

### US
Ariana Grande storms to #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 this week with "Hate That I Made You Love Me," a surprising cold debut that dethrones Drake's "Janice STFU" (now at #3). Ella Langley is the chart's dominant force of 2026, occupying both #2 with "Choosin' Texas" — which has now logged 10 non-consecutive weeks at #1 this year, setting a record for the longest-running #1 by a female country artist on the all-genre Hot 100 — and #4 with "Be Her." Bruno Mars maintains a steady presence at #5 with "I Just Might," while the boundary-crossing Tame Impala and K-pop idol JENNIE collaboration "Dracula" hits its peak at #10, signaling growing crossover appeal between alternative rock and K-pop fandoms.

### EU
The UK chart this week (ending June 11) echoes transatlantic trends, with Ariana Grande's "Hate That I Made You Love Me" claiming the top spot simultaneously on both sides of the Atlantic. Sam Fender's melancholic indie duet "Rein Me In" with Olivia Dean continues a remarkable 51-week chart run at #2, cementing its status as one of the most durable British singles in recent memory. Olivia Rodrigo's emotional pop-rock track "The Cure" holds at #3, while Drake's cross-genre appeal keeps "Janice STFU" at #4. Michael Jackson's catalog classic "Billie Jean" re-enters at #5, reflecting an ongoing streaming resurgence for legacy pop acts.

### CN
China's NetEase Hot Songs chart this week is headlined by indie folk artist 马也 (Ma Ye/Crabbit) with "海屿你" (Hai Yu Ni / "The Sea and You"), a poetic ballad built around ocean imagery that has resonated strongly with younger listeners. The chart reflects a mix of indie and mainstream: 加木's "两难" (Two Dilemmas) continues its viral Douyin-fueled climb on the back of emotionally relatable lyrics about difficult choices, while veteran R&B star 李荣浩's "恋人" (Lover) demonstrates that established artists retain strong streaming power. The continued presence of independent and semi-independent voices at the top signals the growing influence of the indie scene on China's mainstream streaming platforms.
